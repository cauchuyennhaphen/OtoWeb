Strategy

Sustainable success is based on long-term vision and innovative strategy. A clear commitment to electric mobility is essential to Audi's strategy. Within the next five years, the company will expand its EV portfolio: By 2027, the four rings plan to offer a fully electric vehicle in all core segments. Audi is accelerating its transformation into the leading provider of sustainable e-mobility.

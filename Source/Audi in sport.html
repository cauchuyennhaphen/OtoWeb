Audi in sport

Audi has been a close partner and sponsor of regional, national and international sport for decades, whether in the area of developing talent or in cooperation with established sportsmen and women or clubs. Audi supports people who pursue their vision. We provide fresh impetus and open up new horizons.

Audi in motorsport

Vorsprung durch Motorsport Since 1981, Audi Sport has stood for “Vorsprung durch Technik” in motorsport. Audi is currently preparing to enter Formula 1, and Audi Sport customer racing models compete worldwide.

Audi in Football

Audi and FC Bayern: on the ball together since 2002 The partnership between Audi and FC Bayern was established in 2002. The driving force behind this successful collaboration is the absolute will to win, the goal of being the best in one’s class.
